Stray:

is a highly anticipated adventure game that offers players a unique perspective by putting them in the role of a stray cat navigating a futuristic cybercity. Developed by BlueTwelve Studio, the game presents a captivating world filled with intriguing mysteries and challenges. In addition to its visually striking world, "Stray" also promises engaging gameplay mechanics. As the cat protagonist, players will need to use their feline abilities to navigate the environment, solve puzzles, and interact with various characters they encounter along the way. The game's open-world design encourages exploration and discovery, with hidden secrets waiting to be uncovered in every corner. Another aspect that has captured the attention of players is the game's intriguing narrative. "Stray" weaves together a story filled with mystery, adventure, and heartwarming moments. Through their journey, players will uncover the secrets of the cybercity and forge unexpected friendships with both humans and robots.
